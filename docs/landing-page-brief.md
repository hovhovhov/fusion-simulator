# Landing Page Brief (Next Iteration)

This brief defines the static landing page that will replace in-simulator onboarding.

## Purpose

Teach users what fusion is, why it matters, and where current programs stand, then route them to the simulator at `/simulator`.

## Proposed Structure

### 1) Hero

- Full-viewport dark premium section
- Cinematic tokamak still/passive motion
- Tagline: "Fusion energy, simulated."
- Sub-tagline: "From JET's record to DEMO's promise - explore every watt."
- CTA: "Open the simulator ->" linking to `/simulator`

### 2) Where We Stand

- Five cards or timeline for: JET, ITER, SPARC, ARC, DEMO
- Per card: name, year, tagline, paragraph, one key stat
- Reuse simulator preset narrative content

### 3) Why Fusion Matters

- Three columns:
  - Limitless fuel (deuterium/lithium sources)
  - No long-lived waste (relative to fission)
  - No meltdown risk (reaction stops without confinement)

### 4) Why It's Taking So Long

- Three concise paragraphs:
  - Physics challenge
  - Materials challenge
  - Funding/industrialization timeline challenge

### 5) Final CTA

- "Build your own fusion plant"
- Button to `/simulator`

## Design Language

- Same dark premium visual language as simulator
- Same typography: Inter + JetBrains Mono
- Same accent palette (cyan, green for positive states)
- Generous whitespace and calm pacing
- Subtle scroll fades only (no heavy parallax effects)

## Scope Constraints

- Static marketing page only
- No interactive education flow in landing page
- All interactive exploration remains in `/simulator`
